About DiziQuote

DiziQuote is a Turkish series quotes app created for fans of Turkish dramas (Diziler) who love emotional, romantic, motivational, and meaningful dialogues from popular TV series.

Explore the most memorable Turkish drama quotes, beautifully translated and presented in multiple languages so you can enjoy them in your preferred language.

Key Features: Multi-language support, favorite quotes, share quotes, light & dark mode, minimal UI, fast & lightweight.
